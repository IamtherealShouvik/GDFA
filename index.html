Aim of the Project

We're dedicated to revolutionizing biometrics and forensics by seamlessly blending fingerprint research with cutting-edge digital technology. Our mission is to redefine these fields, ushering in innovation and efficiency. With our groundbreaking project, we bridge traditional investigative methods with the digital realm, crucial in complex scenarios like forensic investigations. Our battle-tested algorithms extract valuable information even from faint remnants from leftover fingerprints, saving time and unlocking new dimensions in solving cases.

Additionally, our approach determines the suspect's gender, reducing search time by up to 50% and accelerating investigations. Furthermore, our technology extracts relevant crime records for pre-registered criminals, providing invaluable insights for law enforcement. Our commitment is to deliver efficient solutions, exceeding expectations, and creating enduring value for our partners and clients.
Our workflow is as follows:

Upload

Initiating the process couldn't be more straightforward. Begin by uploading an image of a fingerprint, which you have stored on your local device, into the specified dropbox.

Following this, you can either click on the "Enter" button or simply press the "Enter" key to proceed. Remember that the image formats are restricted to JPG, JPEG, BMP & PNG only.

Scan

Sit back and relax while we deploy an intricate blend of mathematical calculations, diverse algorithms, and advanced neural network strategies.

This powerful combination is meticulously engineered to process the image you've uploaded, ensuring the delivery of the most accurate results in the shortest possible time.

Result

There you are !!  That's precisely what you came here for. After an exhaustive search process and meticulous comparison with our extensive database, our website presents you with the gender of the individual associated with the fingerprint image you uploaded. While our results may occasionally diverge from your expectations, rest assured, our conclusions are drawn from precise and reliable analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the aim and use of the project ?

The aim of this project is to deliver an unique approach of determining the gender of a person, based on the image of the remnants of a fingerprint found from any area.

What are the factors being focused on and studied ?

The project is developed based on 5 fundamental aspects of a fingerprint, which include
• Fingerprint Ridge Density
• Ridge Count
• Ridge Thickness to Valley Thickness Ratio (RTVTR)
• Ridge Width
• Various patterns from different fingerprints.

How can I believe that my identity isn't sold ?

It is a general issue not to believe anyone on the internet, especially in places of entering personal data. We thought of this and came up with a solution. Every image that you upload in our website, is temporarily worked on and then removed from our system. We do not share your fingerprint data with third parties. If we use data for research, it is anonymized and aggregated to ensure privacy. Moreover, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices to ensure that your data is securely transmitted and processed.

What is the accuracy percentage of the algorithms implemented ?

While our models are trained on extensive datasets to maximize accuracy, no automated system is perfect. Our website provides an estimation based on patterns recognized by our algorithms, accuracy can vary between 95.1% - 98.6%.

What is the future of this project ?

Our long-term goal is to continuously improve both the technology behind our fingerprint analysis and the overall user experience on our website. Simultaneously, we aim to refine the user interface and interaction to ensure our website is accessible, intuitive, and informative for all users.  This dual focus on cutting-edge technology and user-centric design is at the heart of our roadmap for the future, as we strive to provide a service that is not only technologically innovative but also ethically responsible and user-friendly.
